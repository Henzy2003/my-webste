Governor of Plateau State

Barr. Caleb Mutfwang

Caleb Manasseh Mutfwang is a Nigerian lawyer and politician who has served as the Executive Governor of Plateau State since May 29, 2023. He is also a former Chairman of Mangu Local Government Area.

As the 6th Executive Governor of Plateau State, I am deeply honored to lead and serve this great state. Alongside my Deputy Governor, Hon. Josephine Piyo, we are committed to uniting the good people of Plateau and steering the state toward inclusive growth and sustainable development.

In accordance with the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, we recognize that peace and security are essential foundations for progress. We will prioritize the restoration of security, confront criminal activities, and implement a comprehensive resettlement plan for internally displaced persons (IDPs).

Our administration will focus on key sectors including Agriculture, Education, Mining and Mineral Resources, the Digital Economy, Youth and Women Empowerment, and Social Development. We will uphold the principles of fairness, equity, and justice across all government policies and will maintain a collaborative relationship with the legislature and judiciary.

While we acknowledge the challenges ahead, we draw inspiration from our founding fathers and past leaders. We are fully committed to delivering tangible and impactful change across Plateau State.

Together, let us build a peaceful, prosperous, and united Plateau for all.

About

Governor Caleb Mutfwang is a passionate leader dedicated to the progress of Plateau State. With extensive experience in both public service and the private sector, he brings a unique perspective to governance. His journey, rooted in a love for education and community service, is focused on creating sustainable development and fostering peace. Governor Mutfwang’s leadership is marked by transparency, integrity, and a commitment to improving the lives of every citizen in Plateau.

Mission

Governor Caleb Mutfwang is on a mission to make Plateau State a model of growth and unity. He is committed to improving governance, infrastructure, and education, while promoting peace and socio-economic development. His focus is on creating equal opportunities for all citizens, bridging gaps in health and security, and building a thriving environment for businesses and communities to grow together.

Vision

Governor Caleb Mutfwang envisions a Plateau State where every citizen, regardless of background, has the chance to succeed. His vision is of a state known for economic prosperity, environmental sustainability, and social harmony. Governor Mutfwang sees a future where the youth are empowered, elders are respected, and the state stands as a beacon of unity and progress for all.





Governor Mutfwang swears- in chief of staff, Advicer on political affairs, others.


The Executive Governor of Plateau State Barr. Caleb Mutfwang has sworn-in the newly appointed Chief of Staff, Mr. Jerry Satmak, Deputy Chief of Staff alongside four of his Special Adviser including Chairman and Members of the Plateau State House of Assembly Commission.

He admonished them to see their appointments as a responsibility and trust bestowed on them by Plateau citizens and must give their best for the success of the administration and the collective growth of Plateau State.

The Governor stated this on Friday during the swearing-in ceremony held at the Victoria Gowon Multipurpose Auditorium, Government House Little Rayfield in Jos the State Capital.

He equally explained that the committee constituted to review the recent employment into the civil service by the outgoing administration is not to witch hunt anybody.

“Let me say this quickly; those who have been appointed to serve in the committee to review the issue of appointment, that we do not intend to witch-hunt anyone.

“As a matter of fact, the information that we based our action on are coming from citizens and we thought it will be irresponsible not to investigate such complaints.

“We will continue to do all we can to ensure that the process is seamless, fair and just. We won’t threat anyone in isolation and I can assure you that no one will be victimized.”

He assured those who have been victimized in the civil service, for one action or the other of good intention of government to address the issues squarely without intimidation. And urged citizens of the state to continue to pray for government as it unfolds policies that are beneficial to citizens.

Speaking on behalf of the appointees, the Special Adviser on Political Affairs, Hon. Latep Dabang, appreciated the Governor for choosing them to serve in their respective capacity.

He assured Governor Mutfwang that himself and other officers would bring to bear their wealth of experience to add value to the administration with a view to ensure the requisite development that Plateau people are yearning for over the years is actualize.

News and Events



1. "Governor Caleb Mutfwang's Commitment to Education: A New Era for Plateau Students"

Governor Caleb Mutfwang has always believed that education is the foundation for a brighter future. His recent announcement of a comprehensive education reform program is setting new standards in Plateau State. The initiative will focus on improving school infrastructure, training teachers, and providing scholarships for deserving students. Governor Mutfwang’s vision for the state's education system includes the integration of technology in classrooms and the expansion of vocational training, ensuring that students are equipped with the skills needed to succeed in today’s world. With a strong commitment to the youth, he is determined to create an education system that empowers the next generation of leaders and innovators.


2. "A New Approach to Healthcare: Governor Mutfwang's Health Initiative"

Governor Caleb Mutfwang recently launched a state-wide healthcare initiative aimed at improving the health and well-being of Plateau State residents. The program will increase access to healthcare services, particularly in rural areas, by expanding healthcare facilities, providing medical supplies, and improving the quality of healthcare workers’ training. Governor Mutfwang’s initiative also focuses on raising awareness about preventive healthcare and promoting healthier lifestyles. The program is designed to ensure that every citizen, regardless of their location, can access quality healthcare. This health initiative demonstrates his dedication to building a stronger and healthier Plateau State for all.


3. "Governor Mutfwang's Vision for Economic Development: Unlocking Plateau’s Potential"

Governor Caleb Mutfwang’s vision for Plateau State includes unlocking the full potential of its economy. In his recent address, he outlined plans to attract investment and create jobs through key infrastructure projects, agriculture development, and the promotion of local businesses. His administration is focused on making Plateau a business-friendly state, offering incentives to investors and entrepreneurs. Governor Mutfwang has also prioritized the development of the tourism sector, leveraging the state's rich natural beauty and culture to attract visitors. By creating an environment where businesses can thrive and citizens can prosper, Governor Mutfwang is committed to making Plateau State a model of economic success.
